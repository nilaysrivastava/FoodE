# FoodE - A Food Ordering Website

Welcome to FoodE, a food ordering website that offers a convenient platform for exploring a wide variety of delicious food items and placing orders effortlessly from the comfort of your home.

## Features

- **Online Food Ordering**: Explore a wide variety of delicious food items and place orders effortlessly from the comfort of your home.
- **User Authentication**: Secure user signup and login functionality ensures a personalized and safe browsing experience for all users.
- **Orders, Cart, and Checkout**: Seamlessly manage your orders, add items to your cart, and proceed to checkout with ease, all within the FoodE platform.
- **Category-Wise Food Items**: Discover and browse food items conveniently categorized to suit your preferences and cravings.
- **RestAPI**: Building scalable and flexible APIs, enabling seamless communication between frontend and backend components.

## Technologies Used

- React.js: Employed for building the frontend of the web application, offering a dynamic and interactive user interface.
- TypeScript: Utilized for adding static typing to JavaScript, enhancing code quality and developer productivity.
- Bootstrap: Utilized for responsive design and layout components, ensuring compatibility across different devices and screen sizes.
- MongoDB: Employed as the NoSQL database for storing and managing food items, user information, orders, and other data.
- Node.js: Used for backend server-side logic and handling of HTTP requests.
- Express.js: Employed as the web application framework for Node.js, facilitating the creation of RESTful APIs and middleware.
